# Your AI Glasses — Quick Guide

These glasses are your hands-free assistant. Just talk to them.

## Starting Up

1. Put on your glasses (or open the VisionClaw app on your phone).
2. Tap the **sparkle button** to start a session.
3. Wait for the chime — you're connected.
4. The assistant will automatically tell you what's on your schedule today.

---

## Things You Can Say

### Email
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "Check my emails" | Reads your unread emails aloud |
| "Reply to that and say I'll call tomorrow" | Sends the reply — confirms first |
| "Send an email to John saying the report is ready" | Drafts and sends the email |

### Schedule
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "What's on my plate today?" | Reads your full day back to you |
| "Remind me to call the accountant Friday at 2pm" | Adds it to your Google Calendar |
| "Add dentist appointment next Tuesday at 10am" | Creates the calendar event |

### Messages to Family
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "I'm heading home" | Texts family your ETA automatically |
| "Tell my wife I'm done at the store" | Sends her that message |
| "Text my son when I leave here" | Fires a message when you walk away |

### Parking
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "Remember where I parked" | Saves your GPS location and texts it to you |

### Notes & Reminders
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "Remember this" (while looking at something) | Saves a photo + note |
| "Don't forget to pick up dry cleaning Thursday" | Creates a reminder |
| "What did I capture today?" | Reads back your notes |

### Meetings
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "I'm in a meeting" | Turns on Do Not Disturb |
| "I'm done with my meeting" | Turns Do Not Disturb off |
| *(automatic)* | 5 minutes before a meeting, you'll hear a prep summary |

### Safety
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "I'm going for a walk, check in at 7pm" | Texts family if you don't check back in |
| "I'm back" or "Cancel check-in" | Cancels the timer safely |

### End of Day
| Say this | What happens |
|---|---|
| "I'm done for the day" | Texts family, reads day summary, turns off Do Not Disturb |

---

## Tips

- **Speak naturally.** No exact phrases needed — just say what you want.
- **It can see what you see.** Hold the camera toward something and ask about it.
- **It confirms before sending.** For emails and important messages, it reads back
  what it's about to send and asks first.
- **Ask "What can you do?"** anytime.

## If Something Doesn't Work

- Make sure the phone has Wi-Fi or cellular data.
- Make sure the app is open in the foreground.
- Tap the sparkle button again to restart the session.
- If calendar or email isn't working, reconnect Google under **Settings ▸ Google Account**.
